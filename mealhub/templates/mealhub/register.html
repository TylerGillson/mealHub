What is a Mouth?

Are you a busy and/or cash strapped person in search of an affordable, home cooked meal? If so, then you're a Mouth. Mealhub.com enables mouths interested in purchasing meals to see meals which have been cooked by Chefs in their area. As a Mouth, you can post meal requests that will be seen by Chefs in your area, or you can simply browse for meals that are already available. If you see a meal that looks good, simply click, "Message the Chef" to start up a conversation with the Chef who cooked it. After trying a meal, Mouths are able to rate it based on their eating experience. Mouths and Chefs will arrange the financial details of their transactions on their own terms.

What is a Chef?

Are you interested in monetizing your kitchen and culinary skills? If so, then you're a Chef. Mealhub.com enables chefs interested in selling home-cooked meals to connect with hungry Mouths in their area. As a Chef, you will advertise the meals you are willing to provide, as well as which meals you have currently available. Your meal postings will contain information such as ingredients, number of servings, price, and availability time. If you see a meal request for something you'd like to cook, simply click, "Message the Mouth" to start up a conversation with the Mouth who requested it. Mouths and Chefs will arrange the financial details of their transactions on their own terms.
